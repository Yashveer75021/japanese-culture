Spring Traditions

Spring in Japan is marked by hanami (cherry blossom viewing), the start of the school and fiscal year, and various flower festivals. Families gather for picnics under blooming trees, celebrating renewal and the fleeting beauty of sakura petals. The season emphasizes new beginnings, fresh starts, and appreciation for life's transient moments.
